Multimodal Machine Learning in Healthcare: A Methodological Review of Techniques and their Clinical Applications.

Muntaqim Ahmed, Ruizhe Ma

Integration of deep learning in healthcare has revolutionized the analysis of complex, high-dimensional, and heterogeneous data. However, traditional single-modal approaches often fail to grasp the multi-faceted nature of human health, in which genetic, environmental, lifestyle, and physiological factors interact in complex ways.

Under Review

Evaluating Raw and Decomposed Physiological Signals for Heart Rate Forecasting in Tactical Athletes

Muntaqim Ahmed, Ruizhe Ma

This study evaluates the feasibility of short-term heart rate forecasting from wearable-derived physiological time series in tactical athletes. In particular, it examines whether decomposing signals into trend and residual components enhances predictive performance compared to modeling raw signals.
